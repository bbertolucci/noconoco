 b) Menu
The menu is available to a user connected by pressing the button : or by sliding to the right from the left edge of the screen of your smartphone or tablet.
Le menu comprend des onglets cliquables ou des dossiers
Clickable tabs open a new page.This is a filter to display only needed video.
The "Twitch" icon has the distinction - if we push it - to open the channel Nolife ∞ in a new windows.
The "Chromecast" icons allows to synchronize its categoryType Chromecast and to cast videos to your television from your smartphone or tablet.
The button "Disconnect" allows you to return to the initial login screen.

 c) The video player
A click on a thumbnail in the list or grid provides access to details of the video.
In landscape mode, only the video screen is visible.
In portrait mode, the summary of the video, title, family, rating, comments are visible.
In portrait mode, the buttons Save / Share and Delete are also present.
For the moment the comments are not accessible in all the videos, and are only an image of the Nolife forum.
To start watching a video, you can directly click on the play icon.
You can also set (before starting the video) the quality LQ, MQ, TV, HD, FHD and the language and subtitles to use.
By default these settings are taken from your account on the noco.tv website
The more the quality of the video is high, the more the bandwidth used is important. So be careful if you have a "data limited" package.
To record a video just click on the Register button.
The number of recordable videos is set to 10. (Excluding videos purchased on the website of noco.tv)
The Delete button deletes the selected video.
Videos are saved in the cache folder of the application. You can always delete them by deleting the cache of noconoco
Purchased videos are stored on the SD card (The path is indicated on the player screen)

 d) Chromecast
Chromecast can only be used when you are connected on WIFI.
When pressing the Chromecast button, you must select the Chromecast categoryType you want to use.
Then the menu will close and the TV will display the video selection menu waiting for instructions.
Then you select a video. A popup appears : This is the remote control.
Details of the videos appear on the TV screen.
You press play to start the video.

 e) Playlist and Favorites
From the left menu, you select the "Add or Remove from my Playlist / my favorites".
The menu close and open a new page with Favorites families videos or Playlist videos.
If you select a video, it will add or delete it from your playlist/ favorites.

 f) Nolife ∞ in live
From the left menu, select the icon
The player appears and the video starts automatically.
In landscape mode, only the video screen is visible.
In portrait mode, you can access the chat by logging in with your Twitch account.
Then you can chat with the members present on the chat or simply read posts.

 g) Nolife offline
You will notice after installing noconoco that on your desktop Android, two icons have been added.
 
The first icon allows access to noconoco while connected to internet and synchronizing with your account on noco.tv to view potentially all of noco videos.
The second icon allows access to noconoco offline. So you can see only the previously recorded videos with noconoco.

 h) Parental control
Parental control allows parents to block unsuitable videos for their kids.
The feature is optional, and it is activated only if you select one of the CSA icons, enter a code and push the [Activate] button
To disable parental controls, simply copy the code previously entered. Parental controls will be disabled in case of disconnection of the account noco. (Of course the child should not know the password for the parent's account.)
